I am a Strategic Product Consultant driven by outcomes. My work is centered on one goal: moving the needle.

With a background spanning API services, marketplaces, e-commerce, and conversational AI, I bridge the gap between complex technical stacks and commercial success. My experience is rooted in the high-velocity environment of pre-Series B startups, complemented by high-impact advisory roles for global household names.

As a former founder who bootstrapped a conversational commerce startup to a 6-figure turnover, I bring a unique owner's perspective to every engagement. I don't just suggest features; I build the systems and strategies that deliver measurable unit economics and sustainable growth.

Based in Munich, I partner with international leadership teams to solve their most critical product challenges.
